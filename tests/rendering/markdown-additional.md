## Math

Let's define the Normal distribution $N(x; \mu, \sigma^2)$ as follows.

$$
N(x; \mu, \sigma^2) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \sigma^2}} \cdot \exp\left(-\frac{\left(x - \mu\right)^2}{\sigma^2}\right)
$$
